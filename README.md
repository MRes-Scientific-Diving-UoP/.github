## About the Programme

The **MRes Scientific Diving** programme combines professional HSE SCUBA diving qualifications with postgraduate-level marine research. Based at the **Coxside Marine Station** in Plymouth, students gain internationally recognised diving certifications, undertake a field expedition to **Bali**, and complete a 9-month research dissertation with one of **40+ partner organisations** worldwide.

<div align="center">

| Module | Code | Credits | Focus |
|--------|------|---------|-------|
| **Scientific Diving Theory & Practice** | SCIDIV5003 | 30 | HSE SCUBA, underwater photography, survey methods |
| **Scientific Diving Research Skills** | SCIDIV5002 | 20 | Statistics, species ID, expedition planning |
| **Scientific Diving Field Course** | SCIDIV5001 | 10 | Research expedition to Nusa Penida, Bali |
| **Dissertation Project** | SCIDIV5000 | 120 | 9-month research project with external partner |
