# Code of Conduct

This organisation hosts work by students and staff on the MRes Scientific Diving
programme at the University of Plymouth. We want it to be a supportive, professional,
and welcoming place to learn.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, and ask before assuming a mistake.
- Credit others' data, code, and ideas.
- Keep sensitive data (personal, location-sensitive species, unpublished partner
  data) out of public repositories.

## Scope

This applies to all repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions within the
organisation, and reflects the University of Plymouth's policies on conduct and
academic integrity.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact the Programme Leader,
**Dr Keiron Fraser** — [keiron.fraser@plymouth.ac.uk](mailto:keiron.fraser@plymouth.ac.uk).
Concerns will be handled confidentially.
